Who are we?

Suncraft is a non-profit organisation (NPO) created to reduce the food wasted caused by local major supermarkets and distributors. We help students, young families who might be struggling and people in the community.

How we work

We offer different types of services. You can choose to eat at the resturant or have a great coffee while you working in our workspace. For those who aren't afraid to cook with interesting and varied ingredients, we have the Food Box. We also provide a subscription to collect fresh fruit, vegetables and cupboard goods from the warehouse.

We accept cash and card as a payment for all our services. Everybody can afford fresh, nutritional producs and meals, if you are struggling to pay don't worry, your skills and time work for us.

Food Waste

We understand that food waste is a sensitive subject. That's why we want to help educacate potential, but hesitant, patrons that "food waste" is not just rotten, unusable or expired food.
